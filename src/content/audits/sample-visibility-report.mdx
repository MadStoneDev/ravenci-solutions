A quick note on the AI part, since it is new to most people. When someone
asks ChatGPT or a Google AI overview for a "good physio in Bulimba", those
tools read the plain text and the structured data on your site to decide
whether to mention you. Right now your suburbs and services are mostly locked
inside images and the booking widget, so the AI has very little to go on. The
schema and plain-text fixes above are what change that.

None of this is a rebuild. It is a focused half-day of work on the things
patients actually notice: how fast the site loads, and whether they can find
you in the first place.
